CURRENT SITUATION AND WHAT ARE WE FACING RIGHT NOW IN REAL ESTATE INDUSTRY

REAL ESTATE INDUSTRY

The global real estate market, valued at $217 trillion, is a significant component of global assets.

Unlike stocks and bonds, real estate transactions face liquidity challenges, with slower processing times and less efficient pricing mechanisms.

This often traps capital in less liquid investments, especially in areas lacking domestic investment opportunities.

  • • Foreign real estate investment hurdles persist despite investor interest.
  • •Chinese investors, prominent in U.S. real estate, face obstacles despite large investments.
  • •Absence of global standards and platforms impedes electronic record transmission.
  • •Fragmented systems exist with each country maintaining its own rules and registries.
  • •Outdated land registry systems struggle with international transfers, affecting investor experience.
  • •Informal procedures in certain jurisdictions increase fraud risks.
  • •No single online tool exists for researching, paying, and securing international real estate ownership.
